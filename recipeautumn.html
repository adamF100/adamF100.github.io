Saucey mushy dreams

  • 500 g pasta
  • 350 g chanterelles
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 5 dl cream
  • 100 g parmesan cheese
  • 200 g fresh parsley

    • Boil the pasta and chop the fresh parsley. Chop the onion, cut the mushrooms into smaller pieces. Fry the mushrooms and onion in a dash of golden organic butter.

    Pour the cream into the frying pan and let it simmer with the mushrooms and onions and wait for that delicious aroma.

    Mix the sauce with the pasta, and add the parmesan. Sprinkle some salt, pepper and fresh parsley on top.

    Sweet apples with crunchy oats

  • 4 apples
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 dl oats
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 1,5 tsp cinnamon

    • Cut apples into slices then toss them in a frying pan, smash some butter on top and let them fry for a couple of minutes.

    Add oats and sugar on top of the slices, let it fry while you breakdance in your living room for a while. Then dash a dab of cinnamon on top.

    Serve with some good old fashioned vanilla ice cream for good measure.
