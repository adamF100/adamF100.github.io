Cupcakes with raspberries

  • 100 g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ dl sugar
  • 2 dl milk
  • 4 dl flour
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla suger
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 ½ dl raspberries

    • Preheat oven to 200 Celcius degrees.

    Melt the butter and let it cool down.

    Whisk egg and sugar thoroughly then add the butter and milk.

    Mix flour, baking powder and vanilla sugar. Stir it together with the other ingredients.

    Pour into 12 cupcake tins. Place the raspberries on top.

    Bake the cupcakes for 15 minutes.

    Grilled Corn Cobs (4 people)

  • 4 corn cobs
  • Rock N roll salt
  • Butter

    • Fold the blast of the corn cobs out and pull it back. Let it stay, it will serve as a handle when you want to turn the corn on the grill.

    Remove the “beard” from the corn cobs. Grill the corn at a pretty hot barbeque grill under a lid.

    When the corn starts to pop it´s ready. Serve the corn cobs whole or chop them into big pieces and serve with rock salt and butter.
