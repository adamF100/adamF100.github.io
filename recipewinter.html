Blueberry smoothie

  • 2 bananas
  • 4 dl frozen blueberries
  • 4 dl milk
  • 1 dl vanilla yoghurt
  • 1/2 tps honey

    • Mix all the ingredients thoroughly

    Poor them into gigantic glasses

    If you want something extra: serve them with vanilla ice cream on the top!

    Food hack alert!

    Pick blueberries in the summer and freeze them to get a blueberry blast in the winter!

    Hot beets with feta cheese

  • 800 g peeled beets
  • Fresh thyme
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 150 g feta cheese

    • Preheat oven to 200 Celcius degrees. Cut the beets into chunks

    Put them in a baking tray and mix with thyme, garlic salt and pepper.

    Bake them for 30 minutes until they get soft.

    Drizzle oil over the beets and sprinkle with the feta cheese
