Raunchy rhubarb compôte

  • 500 gram rhubarb
  • 1,5 dl water
  • 1,5 dl sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla sugar
  • 1,5 tbsp potato flour

    • Chop the rhubarb into small chunks. Boil them together with the white sugar, vanilla sugar and good old fashioned tap water. Boil until the rhubarb is soft.

    Mix the potato flour with some cold, fresh, hydrating water, add it to the rhubarb mixture and let it simmer for a couple of minutes.

    Serve the awesome cômpote with warm milk and enjoy it with your cat in the glorious spring sunshine. Mmm..

    The asparagus awakening

  • 4 kilos sexy asparagus
  • A bunch of fresh parsley
  • A bunch of chives
  • 2 dl natural yoghurt
  • 4 garlic cloves

    • Slice the ends of the asparagus off, put them in a big, deep, wide, stainless steal, expensive looking pot and pour boiling water over. Be proud of yourself for comming this far.

    Boil until soft, check with an unused toothpick if it's soft enough. 2-3 minutes should be enough.

    Let's continue to the saucy part. Chop the herbs, crush the garlic, toss in some salt and pepper, add yoghurt and you're done!
